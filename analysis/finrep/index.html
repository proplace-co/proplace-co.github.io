❓ Market DEFINITION

AI-automated SEC disclosure drafting, benchmarking, and compliance workflows for US public companies filing 10-Ks, 10-Qs, and Section 16 forms. ➜ This market encompasses the $5.4B GRC software industry focusing on US public company compliance. It targets CFOs and Technical Accounting teams, delivering value by reducing the cost and manual labor of SEC filing preparation and legal disclosure drafting.

💬 Our Market THESIS

MARKET INFLECTION : A non-negotiable shift in Generative AI maturity is triggering a platform transition away from legacy systems in the $15B Financial Reporting market. A startup that becomes the go-to platform for this new reality, centered on AI-Drafting for compliance, can become the new system of record for the entire industry.

🧠 Our CONVICTION & WAGER on this Market:

🟡 MEDIUM: Our conviction is medium, as the thesis hinges entirely on a question of timing. We believe the AI-native shift in financial reporting is real, but the adoption curve is nascent and its velocity is unknown. Our wager is that the market has just crossed the inflection point, making this the precise, narrow window to enter before the opportunity is either premature or too crowded by legacy pivoters.
