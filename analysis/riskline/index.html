Market DEFINITION

The Travel Risk Management (TRM) SaaS market provides real-time destination intelligence, traveler tracking, and emergency alerting to ensure corporate Duty of Care compliance. It operates at the intersection of Travel Tech, Corporate Security, and Insurtech, serving global TMCs and enterprise security departments.

Our Market THESIS

MARKET INFLECTION: A non-negotiable shift in Regulatory Duty of Care mandates is triggering a platform transition away from legacy systems in the $2.5B Travel Risk Management market. A startup that becomes the go-to platform for this new reality, centered on API-first real-time data, can become the new system of record for the entire industry.

Our CONVICTION & WAGER on this Market:
